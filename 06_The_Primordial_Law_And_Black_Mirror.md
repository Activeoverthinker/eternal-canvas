Before civilization. Before language. Before the first lie. Before money. Before status. Before the Machine.

**But the Primordial Law never went away.** It's still there. Written in your DNA. Activated by oxytocin. Felt in your gut. Recognized when you see genuine kindness and your whole body responds.

- Work fully automated, but prosperity concentrated

- Biological humans struggle to compete with AI in most domains
- Gene editing creates biological class divisions
- Brain-computer interfaces blur the line between human and machine
- Corporate-states replace nation-states
- Privacy extinct
- Autonomy extinct
- Dissent algorithmically suppressed before it can organize
- The Primordial Law forgotten entirely, connection becomes a historical curiosity

- Humanity splits into enhanced (augmented, connected to systems) and "naturals" (second-class citizens)

- AI systems make most decisions; humans ratify them without understanding
- The concept of "human nature" becomes obsolete
- Consciousness itself commodified and manipulated
- The last generation to remember what genuine connection felt like dies out

**This is not hyperbole.** Every trajectory is already in motion. Every technology exists. Every incentive structure is in place. **This is where we're going unless we consciously choose otherwise.**
